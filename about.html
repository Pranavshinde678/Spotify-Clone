About the Spotify Clone

Discover the Spotify Clone, your go-to platform for a streamlined and immersive music experience. This application emulates Spotify's core features, offering a sleek and intuitive interface to enjoy your favorite tracks.

Key Features

Playlists & Tracks

Browse through a carefully curated list of 10 songs, each organized into playlists for easy access and enjoyable playback.

Playback Controls

Control your music effortlessly with user-friendly play, pause, and skip buttons.

Volume & Progress Bar

Adjust your listening experience with precise volume and progress controls.

Interactive Elements

Enjoy a responsive and engaging interface with real-time song information and dynamic buttons.
